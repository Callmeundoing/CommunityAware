The Sacrifices & Benefits

As an environmentalist, we should remove the dams. The dams have caused the temperature of the river water to rise, which can be conflicting for salmon when they move since they are sensitive to the change in water. Some areas have confused salmon while they are moving against a current. Modification benefits us because fish ladders being installed allow the salmon easier passage to travel. The cost is that some issues remain, but the changes made will be beneficial, making it a good beginning in fixing the issue.













What The Modification Means To Us

Modification of the dam does not fully benefit us as environmentalists, but modification allows fish ladders to be installed, which when effective, allow full passage to salmon across the dam. This is an essential foundation to save the environment.
