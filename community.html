The Sacrifices & Benefits

Preserving the dams allows us to maintain cheap electricity costs. We can also continue to have recreational activities in the water and surrounding areas. However, we must compromise so other stake holders can get their way. Although there are not direct costs and benefits for us, it helps many other stakeholders, making our community better.













What The Modification Means To Us

The modification of the dam does not affect us as the dam still provides sufficient energy generation for cheaper electricity costs. We also continue to maintain our recreational activities in and around the dams. Making modification a suitable sollution to help solve other stakeholder issues.
